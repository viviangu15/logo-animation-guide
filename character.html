Micro Expressive Character
Character — Hands Concept
Two small dots at the sides act as hands. Combined with eye animations, the logo gains gesture — it can wave, point, droop, celebrate. Same silhouette, much more personality.
Thinking
Eyes round out. Right hand grows, rises to chin.
Focused, patient
Searching
Eyes widen + flatten (alert). Hands shade brow.
Curious, methodical
Success
Eyes become wide ovals. Hands grow big + rise up.
Satisfied, positive
Failed / Error
Eyes flatten into sad slits. Hands shrink + droop.
Apologetic, composed
Needs Input
Blinks. Right hand waves gently.
Polite nudge, attentive
Wave
Wide eyes. Both hands wave enthusiastically.
Warm, inviting
Bored / Idle
Eyes flatten to thin slits. Hands shrink + hang.
Playful, subtle
Typing / Working
Eyes steady. Hands alternate grow-tap rhythm.
Neutral, rhythmic
Celebration
Wide oval eyes. Hands grow big + thrown way up.
Warm, rewarding
Loading
Eyes + hands become dots scrolling through the frame.
Patient, mechanical
Alert
Eyes become an exclamation mark. Hands raise in alarm.
Urgent, needs attention
