Micro Expressive Character
Character — Hands Concept
Two small dots at the sides act as hands. Combined with eye animations, the logo gains gesture — it can wave, point, droop, celebrate. Same silhouette, much more personality.
Thinking
Eyes gaze up. One hand rises to chin.
Focused, patient
Searching
Eyes dart. Hands follow the scan direction.
Curious, methodical
Success
Happy eyes. Both hands thrown up.
Satisfied, positive
Failed / Error
Droopy eyes. Hands sag down.
Apologetic, composed
Needs Input
Blinks. Right hand waves gently.
Polite nudge, attentive
Wave
Wide eyes. Both hands wave enthusiastically.
Warm, inviting
Bored / Idle
Eyes close. Hands hang low and limp.
Playful, subtle
Typing / Working
Body bounces. Hands tap alternately.
Neutral, rhythmic
Celebration
Happy eyes. Hands thrown way up. Body jumps.
Warm, rewarding
